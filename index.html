Majed's Satellite Project

This website aims at building a satellite project for the One More Voice project. It is a part of the ENGL877 Advanced Digital Humanities course offered by the English Department at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. The focus of this website will be the Indian female writer and social reformer Cornelia Sorabji.
